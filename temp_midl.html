What is Midl?

An Execution Environment that brings smart contracts to Bitcoin.
You sign a regular Bitcoin tx;
You use regular BTC & native assets;

No bridges, no extra steps. Fully native UX.

Is Midl a Layer2?

No! Midl works like an External VM for BTC. L2s batch elsewhere and make you bridge;

Midl executes internally and finalizes on Bitcoin.

Why be early with Midl?

Bitcoin never had the true token economy. Without dApps, native tokens had no utility.
Before Midl.

You're not early to Midl, you're early to Bitcoin.

Who can build with Midl?

Any EVM-familiar team.
Midl offers DevEx similar to one devs expect with familiar tooling and rules

Yet still tapping directly into Bitcoin.

How does Midl stay tied to Bitcoin?

To exist on Midl, a tx should always exist on Bitcoin. Users do only BTC txs;

Validators execute the logic and reflect the outcome.

Zero Friction experience for everyone.

Is Midl Live?

Midl is live on the BTC testnet. Breakthrough dApps are already deployed.

Mainnet launches once the performance is fully validated & audits are completed.
